KOUFALL Terracotta Curtains

These KOUFALL Terracotta Curtains effortlessly combine rustic charm with a soft, elegant light effect. Created with a textured linen finish and a subtle sheen, they add a warm and inviting atmosphere to any room while maintaining breathability and freshness. The sheer yet durable fabric allows natural light to pass through gently, creating a peaceful and cozy ambiance that enhances your living space.

Ideal for living rooms, sliding doors, and French dining windows, these curtains offer a versatile design that complements various home decor styles. Whether you're looking for a timeless country look or a modern farmhouse aesthetic, they adapt seamlessly. Each pair comes with 2 panels and is crafted to fit standard window sizes with a 1.5 inch header and 3 inch rod pocket, ensuring easy installation and a clean, finished look.
