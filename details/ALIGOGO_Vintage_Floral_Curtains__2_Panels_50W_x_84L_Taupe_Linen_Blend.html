Elevate Your Space with ALIGOGO Vintage Floral Curtains

Bring a touch of timeless beauty and rustic charm to your living room or entrance with these handcrafted vintage floral curtains.

Timeless Design, Modern Comfort

These ALIGOGO vintage floral curtains are a perfect blend of classic style and practical function. The floral pattern is inspired by antique toile designs, adding a serene, natural allure to your farmhouse or cottagecore decor. Each curtain is made from a premium blend of 20% linen and 80% Polyester, offering a soft texture that feels cozy and comfortable while maintaining the durability you need for daily use.

Perfect Fit, Effortless Installation

Consisting of two 50-inch wide by 84-inch long panels, the set provides full coverage with a total width of 100 inches, ideal for wide windows and grand entrances. The 8 nickel grommets on each panel make hanging and adjusting the curtains a breeze, delivering a refined, elegant finish that complements your home's overall aesthetic. Semi-sheer material allows natural light to flow in while still creating a private, inviting space.

Why Choose Our Vintage Floral Curtains?

  • 20% Linen & 80% Polyester Blend: This durable and soft fabric combination ensures your curtains stay elegant and easy to maintain.
  • 2 Panel Set (100 Inch Wide): Ideal for wide windows, these curtains provide full coverage without sacrificing style.
  • 8 Nickel Grommets on Each Panel: Effortlessly hang your curtains and enjoy a polished, classic look.
  • Vintage Bohemian Design: The floral pattern brings that rustic, pastoral charm to any room, making it perfect for farmhouse or cottagecore interiors.

Add Rustic Elegance to Your Living Room or Entryway

Whether you're updating your farmhouse style or adding a touch of refined elegance to your space, these vintage floral curtains are the perfect choice for any decor lover.
