Hipiwe 8 Pack Clear Acrylic Display Stands

These Hipiwe acrylic display stands are made from premium, high-quality clear acrylic, offering a sleek and durable way to display your favorite items. Their smooth surface adds an elegant touch while making cleaning and maintenance effortless.

Each stand features a sturdy square base with three pegs, ensuring a secure and stable hold. This makes them perfect for displaying geodes, mineral agate, painted rocks, glass art, and more without the risk of tipping over.

The compact size of 2.5 inches by 2.5 inches is ideal for small collectibles, allowing them to stand out without overwhelming your space. Whether you're arranging home decor, organizing an art class, or setting up a museum exhibit, these stands provide a versatile and stylish solution.

With a pack of 8 included, you can always have backup stands ready for display. These Hipiwe displays are not just functional but also add a touch of sophistication to any collection or presentation.
