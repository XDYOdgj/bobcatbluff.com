Elevate Your Celebration with Hysilla Tassel Garland Set

This exquisite tassel garland set is designed to transform any space into a glamorous and inviting environment. Perfect for weddings, birthdays, and other special events, it brings the perfect mix of elegance and fun to your party decor.

Stunning Color & Design

Featuring a beautiful combination of pink, white, peach, and rose gold hues, the Hysilla garland creates a radiant and eye-catching display. The metallic fringe pattern is designed to catch the light, ensuring your space glows with sophistication and charm.

Easy to Set Up & Use

With a total length of 59 feet, split into four strips for effortless setup, this garland is perfect for large spaces or multiple areas. The pre-strung design simplifies installation, while the lightweight and reusable construction ensures it can be used across multiple events with ease.

Versatile Party Decor

This set is ideal for any party theme, whether it's a birthday, wedding, bachelorette party, or Christmas celebration. The tassels can be spaced evenly or adjusted freely, allowing for a customizable look that suits your event's aesthetic.

Enhance Your Ambiance

Hang it from ceilings, walls, or even as part of your parade float decorations to create a stylish and enchanting atmosphere. Its vibrant and eye-catching design is sure to inspire awe and make your event unforgettable.
