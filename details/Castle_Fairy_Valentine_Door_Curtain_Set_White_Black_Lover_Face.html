Valentine Door Curtain Set

Add a touch of elegance and love to your home with this charming Valentine Door Curtain Set from Castle Fairy. The design showcases a beautiful white and black lover face silhouette, bringing a romantic and artistic vibe to any doorway.

This curtain is crafted from high-quality 100% polyester, ensuring durability and a soft, comfortable feel that's ideal for daily use. The grommet top makes installation a breeze, fitting most curtain rods with a sleek and functional finish.
