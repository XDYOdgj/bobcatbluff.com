Elegant Linen French Door Curtains with Tieback

Upgrade your home with these beautiful, functional linen door panels that blend style and practicality. Designed to bring warmth and sophistication to any space, they offer the perfect balance of privacy and natural light.

Soft & Drapey Linen Fabric

Handcrafted from high-quality flax linen, these curtains provide a soft, elegant drape that complements your decor. The open weave design ensures breathability while maintaining a refined look that enhances the charm of your interior.

Versatile Privacy Solution

With their semi-sheer design, these curtains allow natural light to flow in while offering subtle privacy. Ideal for glass doors and living areas, they help reduce glare without sacrificing style or comfort.

Easy Installation & Maintenance

Featuring 2-inch dual rod pockets at both the top and bottom, these curtains adapt to various window installations. Plus, they are machine washable in cold water, making them a hassle-free choice for families and nurseries.

Charming Tieback Included

Each panel comes with a matching tieback, adding a cohesive and elegant finishing touch to your window treatment. Elevate the aesthetic of your entryways or living room with a seamless, stylish design.

Eco-Friendly & Durable

Made from formaldehyde-free flax linen, these curtains are not only stylish but also eco-conscious and safe for your home. Their durability ensures long-term use without compromising quality or appearance.

Perfect for Any Space

Whether you're decorating a modern or traditional home, these linen door panels are versatile enough to enhance both indoor and outdoor spaces. Their natural aesthetic and elegant design make them a must-have for every room.
