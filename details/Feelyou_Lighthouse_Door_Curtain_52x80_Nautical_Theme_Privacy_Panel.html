Feelyou Lighthouse Door Curtain

Elevate your home decor with this stunning 52" x 80" nautical-themed door curtain. Designed with a detailed lighthouse pattern, it brings a touch of coastal charm to any room, whether it's your kitchen, bedroom, or living area. The curtain is crafted from premium velvet fabric, offering a soft, luxurious feel while providing exceptional blackout insulation to protect your privacy and reduce light intrusion.

With its convenient built-in plastic grommets, this door curtain is easy to install and perfect for quick setup.
