Erosebridal Rustic Farmhouse Door Curtain

Add a touch of rustic charm and cozy style to your home with this beautifully crafted door curtain. Designed with voluminous velvet fabric, it offers a soft, luxurious feel and exceptional privacy, making it perfect for bedrooms, living rooms, or even your guest space.

Featuring a silver plastic grommet design, installation is simple and hassle-free, whether you're tackling a DIY project or working with a professional. This curtain blocks up to 30-50% of sunlight, helping you create a more comfortable and intimate atmosphere in your room, especially during the evening.

Versatile and practical, it can be used on sliding doors, folding doors, and even windows, making it an all-in-one decor solution that works year-round. Its bohemian farmhouse style blends effortlessly with any interior, adding warmth and inviting ambiance to your living space.
